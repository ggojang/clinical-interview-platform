# Interview Pattern Specification

An Interview Pattern is a reusable plan for gathering information around a presentation or clinical interview objective.

It is not a diagnosis.

A pattern declares:

- stable pattern ID and version;
- purpose;
- activation cues;
- required facts;
- optional facts;
- linked safety rules;
- completion criteria;
- provenance.

Pattern activation may be tentative. Multiple patterns may be active simultaneously.

A required fact is required for interview completeness, not necessarily required for diagnosis.
